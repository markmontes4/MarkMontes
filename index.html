About

Hello, my name is Mark Montes. I am a recent Portland State University graduate. At Portland State, our program focuses on the fundamentals of how programs work and expose us to the software engineering role. I've collaborated with many students to build projects for certain courses. I was also SCRUM master for one of these group projects. The SCRUM master is the leading position of the group. This role gave me experience with being a leader and helped developed my communication skills further.

I currently work full time as an IT Administrator at a company called Pentagon Technologies. In this position I have gained expertise in networking computers, managing servers, managing our database management system, writing scripts in various languages JSL and Filemaker Pro, and various administrative skills. A dream job of mine would be one that would allow me travel all around the world so I may gain new experiences' and cultures.
